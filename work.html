The tracks I have taken

01 Community is where it started. Community

Working inside ecosystems taught me how people actually behave inside products: what makes them join, stay, trust, and bring others in. No deck teaches that.

  • Worked across communities including Initia, Mira, Injective, Hyperion, Seismic, Billions Network, and Hemi.
  • Ran moderation, ambassador programs, AMAs, Spaces, online events, and IRL moments.
  • Built a foundation in user psychology, retention, and what makes communities stick.
02 Then I started looking at why products lose people. UX Strategy

At Refine Studio, I worked as a UX Strategist embedded in client products. My job was to find the invisible friction that slows growth before anyone names it.

  • Ran UX audits, user journey analysis, onboarding reviews, and conversion path analysis across client products.
  • Projects included Curvance, Haiku, Symphony, and gmTrade.
  • Delivered feature recommendations, flow improvements, and activation fixes grounded in user behavior, not assumptions.
03 Then I moved from analysis to execution. Growth Ops

I transitioned into a Growth Operator role at Refine Studio, working directly with founders to build systems and move faster. Analysis gave me the problems. Operations gave me the tools to fix them.

  • Built GTM strategies, sales funnels, workflow systems, and internal tooling for multiple products.
  • Built a Content Approval Platform that reduced founder content review from daily to roughly weekly.
  • Built an AI Lead Scouting Tool that reduced manual research effort by 70 to 80%.
  • Contributed to Scout GTM: positioning, messaging, acquisition strategy, and launch planning.
04 Then I helped ecosystems grow. GTM & Partnerships

At Yap Market, I worked across partnerships, GTM, campaigns, and ecosystem growth, sitting between the strategy and the teams executing it.

  • Supported Hemi Network, Income RWA, EmbueFi, and other ecosystem partners on GTM, launch positioning, and acquisition strategy.
  • Closed 15+ strategic partnerships and organized a Network School collaboration in Dubai.
  • Helped projects think through campaign structure, distribution, and ecosystem activation from zero.
Next Now I want to go deeper into product. Product Growth

The through-line across every role has been the same: find the problem, understand the user, build the fix, measure what moved. That is product thinking. I have been doing it without the title.

  • Looking for a product growth, product marketing, or startup operator role where execution depth matters more than job title history.
  • Currently sharpening product analytics, SQL, PM frameworks, and AI-powered automation.
