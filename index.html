Context

Reviewed onboarding internally at Refine Studio — QA, feature recommendations, and simplifying the first-time user experience for a DeFi lending protocol.

What I flagged
  • Too many steps before users could do anything useful
  • Value wasn't surfaced early enough

My Recommendations

  • Surface the top APY on the first screen

UX StrategyUsability Review
Context

Reviewed the multi-asset swap interface — focused on the first-use experience and where new users were getting lost.

What I flagged
  • No obvious starting point — interface assumed familiarity users didn't have
  • Users had to figure out the UX pattern before seeing any value
My Recommendations
  • A "Start your first swap" guided wizard for first-timers
  • Show expected output before users confirm
  • Surface 3–5 popular trading pairs upfront

Trust DesignProduct Strategy
Context

Reviewed trust signals for an AI agent that autonomously trades on your behalf — a product where trust is the entire conversion problem.

What I flagged
  • Users had zero visibility into what the AI was doing or why
  • No way to audit past decisions
My Recommendations
  • Add a decision log with a one-line "why" per trade
  • Let users set a risk cap visibly
  • Show live position rationale in plain language

Benchmarked their perpetual DEX against dYdX and Hyperliquid — identifying where the experience gap was largest.
